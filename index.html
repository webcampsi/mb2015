what is webcamp

WebCamp Ljubljana is an event where Web developers come together to share their knowledge, experience and ideas.

It is a volunteer event organized by community for the community. We expect over 300 attendees from Slovenia and neighbouring countries which is why the event will be held in English.

Topics that will be covered are: Front end, Back end and DevOps.

location

We're once again kindly hosted by the Faculty of Computer and Information Science in Ljubljana. It is the leading teaching and research institution in the field with a number of active research groups, as well as a lengthy roster of distinguished alumni.

call for proposals

We are looking for talks in the following areas:

  • Front end (HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, ..)
  • Back end (Frameworks, Languages, Middleware)
  • DevOps (Virtualization, Orchestration, Practical approaches)
  • .. and any of the related ideas and buzzwords

Format of the talks

  • You have 25 minutes. You should have slides on your computer in offline form.
  • We can all read tutorials online. Present your experiences, war stories - what worked and what didn’t.
  • If you want to show a demo, plan to prepare a short video, that you’ll play.
  • We’ll take submissions for 5 minute, lightning talks, on the day of event.

Timeline

  • Friday, February 13th - Submissions Close
  • Friday, February 20th - You’ll be notified if your talk is accepted
  • Monday, February 23rd - List of talks will be publicly available
